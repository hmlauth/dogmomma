ABOUT

Introducing the one-and-only Megan Lauth - a native Seattlite with a love for animals. Megan has grown up with cats, dogs, birds and gerbils. She has, and continues to be, a reliable caretaker for friends and family. Megan was known for even loving spiders and would gently pick them up, place them safely on a plant and say, "Live a long life, spider."
